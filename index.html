Belgrade

Enjoy culture, food and nightlife in Serbia's vibrant capital

Top three things to do in Belgrade

Go for dinner on the beautiful Skadarlija Street

Enjoy an unforgettable culinary experience in Skadarlija, Belgrade’s historic bohemian street, where live music and authentic Serbian food meet.

Take a stroll through Kalemegdan Fortress

Kalemegdan Fortress is a historic landmark that tells the story of Belgrade, with breathtaking views, charming cafés, and fascinating historical artifacts.

Experience Belgrade’s nightlife

With restaurants, bars, and nightclubs in every corner of the city, the music, lights, and atmosphere ensure there’s something for everyone to enjoy.
